## Why it matters
Knowing the complaint path can reduce stress if a provider dispute needs to move beyond normal support channels.

## Common mistakes
- Waiting too long to document events.
- Skipping the provider's internal process where it is required first.

## Quick checklist
- Keep dates, emails, statements, and names together.
- Use clear timelines when raising a complaint.
