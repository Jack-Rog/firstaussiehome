## Why it matters
Scam patterns can look polished, especially online, and can target people who are actively researching money topics.

## Common mistakes
- Treating urgency as proof of a limited-time opportunity.
- Sharing documents or money before independent checks are complete.

## Quick checklist
- Pause when pressure or secrecy appears.
- Verify identity and licensing through official channels.
