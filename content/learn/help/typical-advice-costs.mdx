## Why it matters
Cost helps explain why personal advice is often positioned differently from self-serve education.

## Common mistakes
- Comparing prices without comparing scope.
- Ignoring whether implementation or ongoing review is included.

## Quick checklist
- Ask for a clear scope and fee explanation.
- Compare one-off and ongoing cost structures separately.
