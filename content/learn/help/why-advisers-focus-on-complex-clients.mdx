## Why it matters
This explains why early-career adults can struggle to find affordable personal advice even when they need education.

## Common mistakes
- Interpreting the gap as a sign that help is not needed.
- Assuming advice supply is evenly distributed across all client types.

## Quick checklist
- Separate education needs from licensed advice needs.
- Keep cost, complexity, and scope in view when comparing support options.
