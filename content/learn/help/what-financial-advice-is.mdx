## Why it matters
Understanding the line between education and advice helps set correct expectations for tools and content.

## Common mistakes
- Treating general modelling as personal advice.
- Assuming an online tool has assessed full personal circumstances.

## Quick checklist
- Look for wording about scope and assumptions.
- Confirm whether a licensed professional is involved.
