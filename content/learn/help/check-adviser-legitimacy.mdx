## Why it matters
Checking legitimacy reduces the risk of relying on someone who is not authorised to provide the service they claim.

## Common mistakes
- Relying on social proof alone.
- Missing the difference between marketing language and licensing status.

## Quick checklist
- Search the official register.
- Keep screenshots or records of what was presented.
