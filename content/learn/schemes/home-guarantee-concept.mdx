## Why it matters
Guarantee pathways can change the deposit comparison range in a first-home model.

## Common mistakes
- Treating a lower deposit requirement as the only cost difference.
- Ignoring caps, slots, or current availability rules.

## Quick checklist
- Review current caps and vacancy or availability notes.
- Keep deposit size and total upfront cost as separate lines.
