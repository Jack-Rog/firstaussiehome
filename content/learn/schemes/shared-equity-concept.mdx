## Why it matters
Shared equity can materially change how ownership and future gain or loss is split.

## Common mistakes
- Looking only at the initial deposit effect.
- Missing future repayment or exit conditions.

## Quick checklist
- Review the exact ownership and repayment structure.
- Keep long-run flexibility questions in view.
