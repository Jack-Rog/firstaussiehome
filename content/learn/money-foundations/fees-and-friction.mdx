## Why it matters
Repeated costs can reduce progress, especially when time horizons are long and balances get larger.

## Common mistakes
- Looking at headline returns without fee impact.
- Missing one-off setup costs in a cashflow plan.

## Quick checklist
- List recurring account and service costs.
- Compare gross and net outcomes in any scenario.
