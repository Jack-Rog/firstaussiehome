## Why it matters
Inflation can reduce real purchasing power, which changes how far savings and wages stretch over time.

## Common mistakes
- Looking only at account balances without adjusting for price changes.
- Treating short-term price moves as a permanent pattern.

## Quick checklist
- Compare growth after inflation, not just before inflation.
- Review living costs that change faster than the average.
