## Why it matters
Biases like overconfidence, anchoring, and present bias can shift day-to-day decisions without much notice.

## Common mistakes
- Treating impulsive patterns as one-off events.
- Copying another person's risk tolerance or lifestyle.

## Quick checklist
- Add a pause before large money decisions.
- Use written rules for transfers and regular reviews.
