## Why it matters
Understanding risk helps frame why some paths feel smoother while others move around more from year to year.

## Common mistakes
- Treating short-term volatility as the only type of risk.
- Assuming a higher return expectation is guaranteed.

## Quick checklist
- Match time horizon with the range of possible outcomes.
- Review downside scenarios, not just central estimates.
