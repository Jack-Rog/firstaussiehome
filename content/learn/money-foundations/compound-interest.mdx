## Why it matters
Compounding helps explain why steady saving habits and long time frames can matter more than short bursts of effort.

## Common mistakes
- Focusing only on the first year of growth.
- Ignoring the drag from fees and inflation.

## Quick checklist
- Compare short-term and long-term outcomes.
- Check whether growth assumptions are realistic.
