## Why it matters
HELP thresholds can affect take-home cashflow and how payslip withholding looks through the year.

## Common mistakes
- Treating an illustrative threshold as a tailored withholding answer.
- Forgetting thresholds can change across financial years.

## Quick checklist
- Review the latest ATO threshold table.
- Keep HELP status details current with payroll and tax records.
