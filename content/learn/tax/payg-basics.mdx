## Why it matters
PAYG helps explain why tax appears on payslips before tax time and why end-of-year outcomes can still change.

## Common mistakes
- Treating withheld tax as the final result.
- Ignoring changes in multiple jobs or other income sources.

## Quick checklist
- Read the tax and super lines on each payslip.
- Keep records of employment changes across the year.
