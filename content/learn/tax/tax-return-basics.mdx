## Why it matters
Tax time can feel less stressful when records are gathered steadily during the year rather than all at once.

## Common mistakes
- Waiting until the last moment to gather payslips and receipts.
- Relying on memory instead of keeping records.

## Quick checklist
- Keep payslips, summaries, and receipts in one place.
- Check that pre-filled information matches personal records.
