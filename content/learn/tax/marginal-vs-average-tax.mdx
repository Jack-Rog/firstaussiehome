## Why it matters
This concept helps explain why moving into a higher bracket does not change the tax applied to every dollar.

## Common mistakes
- Thinking a higher bracket applies to the full income amount.
- Mixing tax tables with take-home pay expectations.

## Quick checklist
- Separate the next-dollar rate from the blended rate.
- Re-check figures when income changes during the year.
