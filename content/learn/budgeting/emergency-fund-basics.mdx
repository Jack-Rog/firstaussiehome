## Why it matters
A cash buffer can help absorb timing shocks, repairs, or short periods of lower income.

## Common mistakes
- Counting the same cash twice for both emergencies and long-term goals.
- Keeping no clear rule for what qualifies as an emergency.

## Quick checklist
- Define what the buffer is for.
- Review how quickly cash can be accessed if needed.
