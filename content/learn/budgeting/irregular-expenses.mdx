## Why it matters
Large annual or quarterly bills can make a budget look healthy until the real due date arrives.

## Common mistakes
- Leaving car, travel, gifts, and insurance out of the monthly plan.
- Treating annual renewals as unexpected.

## Quick checklist
- List known annual, quarterly, and seasonal costs.
- Convert big annual costs into monthly provisions.
