## Why it matters
These terms often appear early in loan research, and they are easy to confuse.

## Common mistakes
- Treating redraw access and offset access as identical.
- Ignoring access rules, fees, or policy conditions.

## Quick checklist
- Review how each feature handles access and interest.
- Keep emergency cash access in mind when comparing features.
