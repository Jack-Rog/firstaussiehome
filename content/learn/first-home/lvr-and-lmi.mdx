## Why it matters
LVR and LMI are common first-home terms that affect how upfront and ongoing costs are discussed.

## Common mistakes
- Treating LMI as part of the deposit itself.
- Assuming the same cost applies across all circumstances.

## Quick checklist
- Note the property value, loan amount, and deposit separately.
- Compare LVR scenarios using broad ranges only.
