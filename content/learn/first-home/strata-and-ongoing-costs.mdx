## Why it matters
Ongoing costs can materially change cashflow after settlement, especially in the first year.

## Common mistakes
- Focusing only on deposit and repayments.
- Missing annual, quarterly, or repair-related costs.

## Quick checklist
- List regular property costs before using a repayment estimate.
- Add a maintenance and contingency line to the budget.
