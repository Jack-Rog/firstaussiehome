## Why it matters
Deposit size affects borrowing ranges, purchase costs, and the pace of a saving plan.

## Common mistakes
- Counting only the deposit and not other upfront purchase costs.
- Comparing a target percentage without looking at the dollar amount.

## Quick checklist
- Track deposit and other upfront costs separately.
- Compare multiple deposit ranges, not just one target.
