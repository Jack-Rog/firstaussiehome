## Why it matters
Settlement involves deadlines, final balances, and practical tasks that are easy to overlook earlier in the journey.

## Common mistakes
- Forgetting adjustments, moving costs, or timing issues.
- Leaving final document checks too late.

## Quick checklist
- Keep a final-stage task list with dates.
- Confirm who handles each task across the legal and finance process.
