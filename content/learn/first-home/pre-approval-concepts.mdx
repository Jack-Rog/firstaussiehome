## Why it matters
Pre-approval can shape timeline planning, documentation, and how quickly a purchase process may move.

## Common mistakes
- Treating a provisional assessment as final certainty.
- Ignoring expiry dates or conditions.

## Quick checklist
- Note what documents may be requested.
- Check how long the provisional assessment remains current.
