## Why it matters
Insurance held in super can change costs and may reduce the amount left invested over time.

## Common mistakes
- Forgetting insurance premiums are being deducted.
- Assuming cover levels stay the same forever.

## Quick checklist
- Review what cover exists and what it costs.
- Check whether the cover remains active after job changes.
