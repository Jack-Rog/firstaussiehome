## Why it matters
Contribution types can affect timing, tax treatment, and what records are useful to keep.

## Common mistakes
- Mixing concessional and non-concessional concepts.
- Ignoring contribution caps and timing rules.

## Quick checklist
- Note the source of each contribution type.
- Review current caps using official references.
