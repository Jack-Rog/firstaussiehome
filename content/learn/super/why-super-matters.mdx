## Why it matters
Super can become one of the largest long-term assets a working adult holds, even though it feels distant early on.

## Common mistakes
- Ignoring super because retirement feels too far away.
- Missing the effect of fees and insurance costs.

## Quick checklist
- Find where current super is held.
- Review fees, insurance, and contributions history.
