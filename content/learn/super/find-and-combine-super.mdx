## Why it matters
Multiple accounts can create duplicate fees or duplicate insurance that is no longer needed.

## Common mistakes
- Overlooking old employer accounts.
- Combining accounts without checking insurance effects first.

## Quick checklist
- Search for all active and inactive accounts.
- Compare fees, insurance, and investment settings before combining.
