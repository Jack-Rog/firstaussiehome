## Why it matters
Diversification can reduce concentration risk when one sector, region, or asset type performs poorly.

## Common mistakes
- Assuming more holdings always means better diversification.
- Ignoring overlap across seemingly different products.

## Quick checklist
- Look for concentration by sector, region, or issuer.
- Review whether holdings move together in similar conditions.
