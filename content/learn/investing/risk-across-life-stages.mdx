## Why it matters
The same asset mix can feel very different when income, debt, or dependants change over time.

## Common mistakes
- Treating age as the only risk input.
- Ignoring the role of cash buffers and near-term spending needs.

## Quick checklist
- Review obligations, emergency cash, and goal timing together.
- Recheck assumptions after major life changes.
