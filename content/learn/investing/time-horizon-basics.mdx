## Why it matters
Short horizons often leave less room to recover from poor market periods, while longer horizons can absorb more movement.

## Common mistakes
- Using a long-run assumption for a near-term goal.
- Ignoring the exact timing of when funds may be needed.

## Quick checklist
- Map each goal to a likely time frame.
- Revisit horizon assumptions when life plans change.
