## Why it matters
Structure affects how units are priced, how transactions occur, and which costs are visible.

## Common mistakes
- Treating all pooled funds as identical.
- Focusing only on labels instead of product documents.

## Quick checklist
- Review fees, liquidity notes, and trading mechanics.
- Compare structure before comparing performance tables.
