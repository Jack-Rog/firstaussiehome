# Compliance

## Guardrails

- Persistent banner on all page routes: general information and modelling only.
- Tier 1 and Tier 2 use non-prescriptive language.
- Tier 3 remains a coming-soon placeholder.
- Tools include assumptions, limitations, and a link to the safety page.
- CI runs a banned-phrase scan.

## Banned phrases

- `you should`
- `we recommend`
- `choose this`
- `choose that`
- `buy this`
- `buy that`
- `switch now`
- `open account`
- `invest in`
- `best lender`
- `best mortgage`
- `apply with`
- `this fund`

## Review rules

- Keep outputs framed as scenarios, ranges, options to compare, and missing-information checklists.
- Link to official sources rather than summarising unstable rules from memory.
- Do not publish product recommendations or lender-specific guidance.
